Does your home get too hot?

This summer, a team of journalists and scientists are working on a community-based project that will show how hot people’s houses really get during a heatwave - and what this means for people’s health. We’re looking for people to take part.

We are working with people in Southwark, in inner London, to place monitors in households during the summer, which will measure heat, humidity and air pollution.
